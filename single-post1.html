【小常識】 鋁門窗保養

By Elon Musk 4 June 2016, Saturday

1.鋁門窗進行清潔時，可用軟布沾清水或中性洗滌劑，不要用普通肥皂和洗衣粉，更不能用去污粉、洗廁精等強酸鹼的清潔劑，這樣會腐蝕型材表面，甚至玻璃、框體等部位都有可能會被劃傷。

2.每半年應對窗扇結合處的溝槽以及滑軌部位進行清埋，防止日後灰塵、沙石等對五金部件的功能造成影響，亦可採用軟毛質地的刷子或吸塵器進行清理。

3.在清潔鋁門窗時，切勿站立在鋁框上，也不要拉扯鋁框作支撐。因為這樣容易造成門窗框架間的磨損甚至意外事故的發生。

4.每半年應對鋁門窗的五金零件進行檢查，及時旋緊螺栓，更換已受損的零件。

5.鋁門窗如因氧化而生鏽時，可用小刀將鋁銹輕輕刮去，再用肥皂水洗乾淨，用乾布抹乾，打上蠟油之後再用乾布擦亮，則更能光滑如新。每半年可使用潤滑油對五金轉動、滑動和螺絲孔等部位進行潤滑滴入潤滑油後反復開合數次，方可保持五金部件的靈活性及增加使用壽命。

6.鋁門窗在使用中，動作要輕，推拉順其自然；在啟閉過程中如發現有阻力時千萬不要蠻力拉扯或硬推，應先排除故障。

7.積灰，變形是鋁門窗啟閉困難的大部分主因，所以鋁門窗框要保持清潔， 特別是鋁窗溝槽的清潔。可用吸塵器吸去槽內的積灰。

1. Aluminum doors and windows for cleaning, you can use a soft cloth stained with water or neutral detergent, do not use ordinary soap and detergent, can not use decontamination powder, toilet and other strong acid detergent, Corrosion profile surface, and even glass, frame and other parts are likely to be scratched.

2. Every six months should be combined with the groove at the groove and slide parts to be buried, to prevent future dust, sand and other hardware components on the impact of the function, can also use soft hair texture of the brush or vacuum cleaner to clean up.

3. When cleaning aluminum doors and windows, do not stand on the aluminum frame, do not pull aluminum frame for support. Because it is likely to cause the doors and windows between the frame wear and even accident occurred.

4. Every six months should be aluminum doors and windows of the hardware parts to check, tighten the bolt in time to replace the damaged parts.

5. Aluminum doors and windows due to oxidation and rust, the knife can be used to gently wipe the aluminum rust, and then washed with soap, dry with a dry cloth, marked with wax and then polished with a dry cloth, the more smooth like New. Every six months can use the lubricating oil on the metal rotation, sliding and screw holes and other parts of the lubrication drops into the oil after repeated opening and closing several times, in order to maintain the flexibility of the hardware components and increase the service life.

6. Aluminum doors and windows in use, the action should be light, push pull its natural; in the process of opening and closing if there is resistance when you do not brute force pull or push, you should first troubleshoot.

7. Fouling, deformation of aluminum doors and windows are mostly difficult to open and close the main reason, so aluminum doors and windows to keep clean, especially the aluminum window groove cleaning. Vacuum can be used to suck the fouling of the tank.
