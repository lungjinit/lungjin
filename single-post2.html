【推薦】 氣密窗和傳統鋁窗差異

By Elon Musk 4 June 2016, Saturday

氣密窗和一般傳統鋁窗究竟有何差異？若以外觀來判斷，兩者基本上是相同的，不同之處在於用料、膠條與五金配件，必須通過國家標準的風雨測試和隔音測試，並擁有合格測驗報告書，才能稱之為氣密窗，也因此與傳統鋁窗價差達近3成之多。 合格的氣密窗一定要通過CNS（經濟部中央標準檢驗局）規範的風雨實驗測試包括水密性50kgf／㎡以上、氣密性2等級以上、抗風壓性在360kgf／㎡以上、遮音 TS30；而具隔音效果的氣密窗，尚須通過室內減低30分貝的測試。民眾在選擇氣密窗時，須注意廠商是否可以提供符合國家標準的測試報告證明書。目前國家認證的合格檢測機構，包括CTC（兆立科技）、台灣大學、成功大學及海洋大學等。 除上述專業性之判斷，一般消費者仍有簡單的方法判定氣密窗的好壞，好的氣密窗關上後，應該要完全聽不到汽車聲、鳥叫聲等高音頻的聲音，並隔絕至少90%的噪音，但或多或少還是會聽到低音頻的聲音，像是公車聲、卡車聲和摩托車等，因為低音頻的聲音甚至連牆壁都能穿透。 氣密窗的好壞關鍵在於密合性，簡易的測試方法為，將名片放在邊框上，並將窗戶關閉後夾緊名片，若沒過多久名片就掉落下來，則其密合度可能不夠。窗戶密合性與氣密膠條有關，常見的PVC材質氣密條，使用久了之後，容易收縮變形，窗框的密合度會變差；而像EPDM橡膠氣密條等較高級的材質，擁有不容易老化、穩定、不易變形等優點，也因此EPDM橡膠氣密條較PVC材質價格高出1倍多。

What are the differences between airtight windows and general traditional aluminum windows? If the appearance to judge, the two are basically the same, the difference is that materials, strips and hardware accessories, must pass the national standard of wind and rain testing and noise testing, and have a qualified test report, can be called gas Dense window, and therefore with the traditional aluminum window spread nearly as much as 3 percent. Qualified airtight windows must pass the CNS (Ministry of Economic Affairs Central Standard Inspection Bureau) standard wind and rain test test, including watertight 50kgf / ㎡ above, air tightness 2 grade above, wind pressure in 360kgf / ㎡ above, And sound insulation effect of the airtight window, still need to reduce the indoor 30 dB test. When selecting the airtight window, the public should pay attention to whether the manufacturer can provide a test report certificate in line with the national standard. At present, the national certification of qualified testing organizations, including CTC (trillion legislation), Taiwan University, University of Success and Ocean University. In addition to the above professional judgment, the general consumer still has a simple way to determine the airtight window is good or bad, good airtight window closed, you should completely hear the car sound, birds and other high-pitched sound Almost at least 90% of the noise, but more or less will hear the sound of low audio, such as bus sound, truck sound and motorcycles, etc., because the sound of low audio and even walls can penetrate. The key to the airtight window is the close, simple test method, the business card on the border, and the window closed after clamping business cards, if not long before the business card fell down, then the degree of adhesion may not be enough The Window adhesion and airtight tape related to the common PVC material airtight, after a long time, easy to shrink deformation, the window frame of the degree of adhesion will be worse; and like EPDM rubber gas and other more advanced materials, With easy aging, stable, easy to deformation, etc., and therefore EPDM rubber airtight material than PVC material price more than 1 times.
